Who We Are

Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation is a diversified downstream energy company specializing in petroleum refining, asphalt, renewable fuels, and logistics.

Our Mission & Vision

Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation is the region's oil and gas industry's flagship, a company with a rich history and traditions that is constantly evolving, a leader in the implementation of process and technological innovations, as well as social responsibility standards.

We create oil! By extracting oil, we contribute to our country's well-being and prosperity, as well as to the realization of shareholders' interests and the development of social partnership.

Our primary purpose is to create value for the Republic of Kazakhstan, our shareholders, the communities where we live and operate, and ourselves.

Our 5 Star Rated Services

Comprehensive energy solutions for a sustainable future

Exploration and Production

One of the Company's strategic goals is to strengthen and increase its resource base. The solid resource base ensures that production operations are maintained and that hydrocarbon output targets are met.

The Company conducts extensive exploratory activities and continues to expand its license portfolio, demonstrating consistent replenishment of oil and gas reserves.

Gas Production

Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation prioritizes gas production and supply while also expanding processing capacity and producing energy at its own power facilities.

To ensure peak performance of connected petroleum gas consumption, we deploy cutting-edge technology in all of our sectors. Our gas processing plant is a modern facility capable of producing both dry stripped gas and liquid hydrocarbons.

Oil Refining

Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation has two commercial divisions: refining and trading. Our collaboration with important refineries in China and Russia resulted in a processing capacity of more than 500,000 barrels per day.

This facility handles operations at the Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation, product supply, bio and intermediate feedstock, and gas logistics.

Oil Marketing

Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation sells crude oil, natural gas, and petroleum products on domestic and international markets, ensuring that flows are appropriately distributed to meet market demand.

The company has crude oil and petroleum product transshipment facilities, as well as pipelines, which contribute to lower transportation costs.
