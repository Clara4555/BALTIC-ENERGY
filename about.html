The Unheard Tales About Us

We are sharpening our focus on meeting the world's growing energy needs while reducing our carbon emissions intensity. The diverse backgrounds and perspectives of our people help make our company a great place to work.

Our Oilfields

Strategic assets powering Kazakhstan's energy future

Baltic Energy Corp. Oilfield

Baltic Energy Corp. is located on the 2,500 square kilometer (1,600 square mile) project license area which includes the super giant Baltic Energy Corp. field, and a smaller but sizable Korolev field and several exploratory prospects.

3.1 billion tonnes
(25 billion barrels) Baltic Energy Corp. field
200 million tonnes
(1.6 billion barrels) Korolev field

Total recoverable crude oil in the Baltic Energy Corp. and Korolev fields is estimated to be 1.4 billion metric tonnes (25 billion barrels) with 200 million metric tonnes (1.6 billion barrels) in the Korolev field.

Baltic Energy Corp. field is one of the world's deepest developed super giant oil fields, with the top of the reservoir at about 4,000 meters (13,000 feet). The reservoir is 20 kilometers (12 miles) wide by 21 kilometers (13 miles) long. The oil column measures an incredible one mile thick.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation

What services does Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation offer?

Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation produces 25% of the country's oil and gas condensate and 15% of natural gas and associated gas. The Company's trunk pipelines transport 56% of oil in Kazakhstan. The share of oil refined by Kazakhstan oil refineries is 81%. In 2021, Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation produced 21,651 thous. tonnes of oil and 8,081 mln m3 of associated gas and natural gas. It transported 74,565 thous tonnes of oil and 81,851 mln m3 of gas from January 1 to November 8, 2021. Oil refineries refined 18,833 thousand tonnes of oil.

How can I request a consultation?

You can request a consultation by contacting our customer service team through our website, email, or phone. Our experts will get in touch with you to understand your requirements and schedule a meeting at your convenience.

What industries do you serve?

We serve a wide range of industries including transportation, manufacturing, power generation, residential heating, and commercial sectors. Our products and services support various economic activities across Kazakhstan and international markets.

How does your safety consulting service work?

Our safety consulting begins with a comprehensive assessment of your operations, followed by customized recommendations based on international best practices and local regulations. We provide ongoing support, training, and monitoring to ensure continuous safety improvement.

Can you help with energy cost reduction?

Yes, we offer comprehensive energy efficiency solutions that help our clients reduce operational costs while maintaining performance. Our experts analyze your energy usage patterns and recommend optimized solutions tailored to your specific needs.

What sets Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation apart from competitors?

Our unique combination of extensive local expertise, international standards, vertically integrated operations, commitment to sustainability, and strong focus on community development sets us apart. We're not just an energy company; we're a partner in Kazakhstan's growth and development.
