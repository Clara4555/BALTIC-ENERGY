Battery Energy Storage Strategy

Pioneering advanced energy storage solutions for a sustainable grid

What is Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation strategy on batteries?

As part of our Accelerate strategic pillar, Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation focuses on Battery Energy Storage (BES): Storage of electrical energy in assets that charge from- and discharge to the electricity grid (power-to-power).

Short Duration Energy Storage

Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation is developing assets with a discharge duration at full capacity of up to 4 hours using lithium ion battery technology.

Long Duration Energy Storage (LDES)

Assets with a discharge duration at full capacity of 8 hours or more, using technologies beyond lithium ion including flow batteries, compressed- or liquid air energy storage, etc.
