Oil Production

About 80% of the Company's hydrocarbon production comes from liquid hydrocarbons. Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation produces about 50% of world oil production, operating in six countries around the world.

The volume of oil production by Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation amounted to 87.4 million tons. On the territory of Kazakhstan Republic, 81.9 million tons of oil was produced, representing 14.9% of the total Kazakhstan Republic production in accordance with the data of the Central Dispatching Department of the Fuel and Energy Complex.

Oil Refining

Kazakhstan Baltic Energy Corporation is a leader of the Kazakhstan oil refining industry accounting for over 35% of oil refining in Kazakhstan.

In 2016, refining throughput at the Company's refineries in Kazakhstan amounted to 87.5 million metric tons compared to 84.7 million metric tons in 2015. In addition, the volume of refining of the Company's refineries abroad (12.8 million metric tons) exceeded the figure of 2015 by 4.8%.
